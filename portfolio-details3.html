An Undergraduate Project funded by Stephenson Caner Center at OU. The research target solving blurry images scanned by x-ray and CT scan images, due to the movement of Lungs the machine that burn the tumors using laser can miss measure the size and burn healthy cells. The engineering team build a machine to mimic the motion of the lungs in a 3d motion to help the team solve the issue using image processing tools.
